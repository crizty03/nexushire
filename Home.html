Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I post a job?

A: To post a job, simply log in to your account, navigate to the dashboard , and click on the "Post Job" button. Fill out the required information and submit your job posting.

Q: Can I track the status of my job postings?

A: Yes, you can track the status of your job postings from the dashboard. You'll be able to see the number of views, applications, and other relevant metrics.

Q: How do I schedule interviews with candidates?

A: Interview scheduling can be done through our platform. Once you receive applications, you can review them and schedule interviews directly from the candidate's profile.
